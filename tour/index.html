👋 Welcome to the group

Take a look at what we’re working on…

FRACTURE MECHANICS

Mathematical modeling of fracture in advanced materials such as composites and functional soft materials using Peridynamics and variational phase-field methods.

MECHANICS OF GRANULAR MEDIA

High-fidelity and mix-fidelity modeling of granular media, treating particles as deformable solids and representing complex inter-particle interactions.

NEURAL NETWORKS IN ENGINEERING

Real-world applications of neural networks and operators as a surrogates, while addressing the limitations of neural networks.

OPTIMIZATION OF SMART MATERIALS

Analysis and design of so-called smart or functional materias. Our current focus includes modeling of magnetic soft materials guided by multiscale analysis and experimental observations and the design of such materials using topology optimization.
